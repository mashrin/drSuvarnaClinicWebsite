Dr. C. R. Suvarna

Dr. Suvarna earned his dental degree (B.D.S) from Nair Hospital Dental College in 1980, now having 40 years of experience in practice. He is extensively skilled in the field of Restorative Dentistry. He has trained at the Dawson Academy for Advanced Dental Study, FL, USA. He specializes in Esthetic restorations, occlusion and occlusal harmony, implant restorations, full mouth rehabilitations, diagnosis and the management of temporomandibular joint (jaw joint) disorders and orofacial pain. Dr. Suvarna focusses on providing comprehensive dental care, integrating function and esthetics, achieving harmony between the jaw joints, muscles, gums and supporting tissues and teeth.
