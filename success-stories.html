PATIENT SUCCESS STORIES

Janet Pringle

South Africa

Dr Suvarna,

Going to the dentist was always a terrifying and fearful experience for me. Dr Suvarna changed that. He has restored my faith in dentists. Growing up in South Africa, we were educated about the importance of dental care from a young age. Annual dental checkups were important, especially in terms of having a healthy body. But they were dreaded experiences!
A dentist’s modus operandi, by its very nature, is both intrusive and invasive. As the patient, you feel particularly vulnerable lying on your back; with your mouth wide open; surrounded by great pieces of equipment that look as though they have come off a Sci-Fi movie set; the sound of a drill is in your ears; while the dentist wields his sharp knife.
Trust is imperative in such a scenario. That is what Dr Suvarna creates.
He is a dentist who demonstrates a high amount of skill; has vast experience and tremendous knowledge. All this is packaged in a pair of velvet gloves. Nothing is ever a challenge or an unpleasant task for him. He always has a welcoming smile to set you at ease.
Dr Suvarna practices in an environment which is very hygienic. His technology is the latest and he constantly keeps abreast of new trends in dentistry by attending international conferences.
He is truly an asset to his profession and a role model for the younger generation of dentists.
I am so looking forward to my next dental checkup with Dr Suvarna.



Janet Pringle
South Africa
