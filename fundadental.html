Warning Signs of Gum Disease

  • Gums that bleed when brushing or flossing

  • Red, swollen or tender gums

  • Gums that have pulled away from the teeth

  • Pus at the gum line where the gums are pressed

  • Teeth that are loose or changing in position

  • Any change in the bite

  • Any change in the fit of partial dentures

  • Constant bad breath or bad taste

    • Note: Patients can have gum disease and not exhibit these symptoms. Visit your dentist as soon as you experience any of these problems.
